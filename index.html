Common Questions

FAQ About Sound Healing

Can sound healing help with stress or anxiety?

Yes, sound healing works by harmonizing the body's vibrational field. Stress and anxiety often arise from energetic imbalances, blocked emotions, or misalignment with one's natural rhythm. When specific frequencies are played, they interact with your energy field, dissolving stagnant energy and restoring flow. Certain sounds can recalibrate your aura, clear emotional imprints, and even shift karmic patterns.

What instruments do you use, and how do they impact the body?

I use a variety of instruments depending on the approach, technique and intention. Tibetan singing bowls for chakra analysis, balance of emotions, and alignment of the nadis. Crystal bowls put you in trance through dissonances and guiding voice, working with higher frequencies. Tuning forks target specific points with powerful instantaneous physical effects. Drums and shakers align you with Earth's vibration for grounding. Chimes and bells work on higher chakras, reprogramming the subconscious and helping with fears and releases.

What's the difference between sound healing and Reiki?

Reiki uses hands and intention to channel universal energy, while sound healing uses frequencies, instruments, and intention. Reiki is subtle, working like a gentle cosmic infusion, whereas sound healing is vibrational, actively shifting energy patterns. In astral terms, Reiki can be seen as light entering the auric layers, while sound healing restructures the entire energetic architecture of the soul's vibrational field.

How often should I attend sessions to see results?

For deep emotional work, once a week until you feel alignment. For stress/anxiety relief, weekly at first, then every 2–4 weeks for maintenance. For spiritual growth, whenever you feel called — sound healing thins the veil between realms.

Is sound healing safe for everyone?

Mostly, yes! However, those with severe mental health conditions, epilepsy, or pregnant women should approach with awareness. Sound can awaken deep subconscious patterns, which can be overwhelming if one is not ready for emotional release.

Do I need to believe in it for it to work?

No, but an open mind enhances receptivity. Sound healing works on a vibrational level, not just a belief system. Think of it like music — whether you "believe" in it or not, a certain song will still make you feel something.

How can I integrate the healing effects into daily life?

Drink water to flush out released energies. Spend time in nature to recalibrate with Earth's frequency. Meditate or journal to process insights. Avoid overstimulation — let the frequencies settle into your aura. Use sound at home (mantras, binaural beats) to maintain your vibrational shift.

How should I prepare before a session?

Set an intention if you struggle with surrendering. Stay hydrated — sound moves through water, and your body is 70% water. Avoid heavy meals at least 2 hours in advance. Be open to whatever emotions arise. If you are sensitive, let me know and we'll do grounding beforehand.

How do you personalize a session?

Each person's energy field has a unique resonance. I scan the aura for blockages, analyze chakra levels, and determine what needs work first. From there I choose instruments targeting specific chakras or emotional states, using different frequencies for grounding, release, or expansion. The session is intuitively channeled with guidance from angels and Divine beings.

Can sound healing be done remotely?

Yes! Sound waves travel beyond physical space into the quantum field. Energy and intention can be sent across dimensions, much like prayers or reiki. The astral body absorbs these frequencies even without physical presence.

How long do the effects last?

Some feel an immediate shift lasting a few days. Others experience gradual transformation over weeks. Deep subconscious changes may surface later, requiring integration.

Can I practice sound healing on myself at home?

Absolutely! Just plain humming vibrates your own cells into balance. You can meditate with binaural beats and solfeggio frequencies. A singing bowl or tuning fork is a great first instrument for inducing trance and improving your spirituality.

Can sound healing help me connect with my higher self?

Yes! Specific frequencies can activate the pineal gland (third eye awakening), strengthen intuitive abilities, and raise your vibrational frequency to access higher realms. The astral world responds to vibration — when you align with higher sound frequencies, you open doors to spiritual wisdom, divine messages, and inner knowing.
